The Origin Story

From Building a Startup to Building a Movement

Back in college, I co-founded Rewaysoft Technologies Pvt Ltd — an e-waste management company. I was deep in the problem: mountains of discarded electronics, toxic supply chains, systems nobody wanted to fix.

"Climate tech can't grow until it's made cool and profitable. Awareness isn't optional — it's the whole game."

That realization changed my path. The real bottleneck wasn't technology — it was perception. Climate solutions existed. What was missing was the storytelling that made people care, share, and buy in.

So I took everything I learned as a founder — the hustle, the research, the urgency — and built rewaywdivyansh: a content brand that explains climate tech in plain, powerful words. Now I channel that founder's lens into content that helps sustainability startups find their audience and grow their digital reach.
